About KRKAI & Founder

EVERY VOLUNTEER IS A FOUNDER

Founder, A Group of Engineering Students

KRKAI was born from a simple belief: every child — regardless of background — deserves access to the technologies shaping the future. A Group of Engineering Students founded KRKAI to bring free robotics education directly to orphanages and government schools. What started as weekend workshops has grown into a movement reaching hundreds of students across Tamil Nadu. The goal is not charity — it is empowerment. Every child trained is a future engineer, inventor, or entrepreneur.
